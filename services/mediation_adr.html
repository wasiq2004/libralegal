Mediation & Alternative Dispute Resolution

Facilitating structured mediation and ADR processes to achieve efficient resolution of commercial, employment, and family disputes outside the courts.

Overview

Libra Legal Consultancy advises clients on mediation & alternative dispute resolution matters with a practical focus on commercial outcomes, procedural clarity, and risk control in the UAE. Whether the instruction involves regulatory navigation, transaction support, or contentious proceedings, we help clients move forward with advice that is structured, responsive, and aligned to the realities of the local market.

Our work typically spans early-stage assessment, document review, stakeholder coordination, and implementation support. We tailor each engagement to the client's sector, operating footprint, and urgency, while keeping the broader legal and regulatory context firmly in view.

Strategic Approach

Our approach begins with a close review of the client's objectives, the governing legal framework, and the pressure points most likely to affect timing, cost, or exposure. From there, we build a clear action plan that prioritises the immediate issues without losing sight of the longer-term commercial position.

Where a matter becomes contentious or regulator-facing, we combine careful preparation with decisive execution. We work alongside management teams, investors, in-house counsel, and external experts as needed, so that the advice remains coordinated and commercially useful at every stage.

Key Practice Areas

  • Pre-dispute settlement strategy and case evaluation
  • Representation in formal mediation sessions
  • Commercial, employment, and family ADR support
  • Without-prejudice negotiation planning
  • Drafting settlement terms and enforcement pathways
  • Escalation planning if ADR does not resolve the matter
  • Multi-party dispute facilitation
  • Hybrid mediation-arbitration strategy where appropriate
