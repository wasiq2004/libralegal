Arbitration & Award Enforcement

Strategic arbitration representation and post-award enforcement across UAE and international forums.

Overview

Libra Legal Consultancy provides comprehensive arbitration and dispute resolution services across the UAE's leading arbitral institutions, including DIAC (Dubai International Arbitration Centre), LCIA-DIFC, ADCCAC, and major international forums including the ICC and LCIA. Our practice encompasses the full arbitration lifecycle—from clause drafting and pre-dispute strategy through to hearing advocacy and post-award enforcement.

We act for corporations, financial institutions, government-linked entities, and high-net-worth individuals in disputes spanning commercial contracts, joint ventures, construction projects, distribution agreements, and investor-state matters. Our practitioners bring deep familiarity with UAE procedural law and the intersecting international frameworks that govern complex cross-border arbitrations.

Strategic Approach

Our approach to arbitration is rigorous and commercially grounded. From the outset, we analyse the strengths and vulnerabilities of our client's position, develop a coherent case theory, and structure proceedings to maximise the efficiency and persuasiveness of the presentation. We invest heavily in early case assessment, evidence gathering, and expert coordination—ensuring our clients are never surprised by developments in the proceedings.

Where enforcement of awards is required, we are experienced in navigating the New York Convention framework, the UAE Federal Arbitration Law (Federal Law No. 6 of 2018), and the DIFC-LCIA protocols. We pursue recognition and enforcement proceedings in onshore UAE courts and DIFC courts, and coordinate enforcement in foreign jurisdictions where necessary.

Key Practice Areas

  • Commercial arbitration before DIAC, LCIA-DIFC, ADCCAC, ICC, and UNCITRAL
  • Construction and infrastructure disputes — delay claims, EOT, LD disputes
  • Joint venture and shareholder disputes in arbitral forums
  • Post-award enforcement in UAE onshore and DIFC courts
  • Emergency arbitrator applications and interim relief
  • Arbitration clause drafting and dispute resolution strategy
  • Recognition of foreign arbitral awards under the New York Convention
  • International investment arbitration (ICSID, BIT-based claims)
  • Asset tracing and attachment in support of arbitral proceedings
