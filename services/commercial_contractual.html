Commercial & Contractual Advisory

Drafting, reviewing, and enforcing commercial agreements with a risk-first approach across supply chains, distribution, technology licensing, and cross-border trade.

Overview

Libra Legal Consultancy's commercial practice advises businesses at every stage of the contract lifecycle—from initial structuring and negotiation through to enforcement and dispute resolution. We act for domestic and international businesses across a broad range of commercial arrangements, bringing rigour and commercial awareness to every instruction.

Our clients include multinationals entering the UAE market, regional trading businesses, technology companies, and service providers operating across GCC jurisdictions. We understand that commercial contracts must be legally sound, commercially workable, and designed to protect against the risks that materialise in practice.

Strategic Approach

We approach commercial advisory with a risk-first lens—identifying the exposures that matter most to our clients and structuring contractual protections accordingly. Our drafting is precise, practical, and tailored to the governing law and jurisdiction of the transaction. Where disputes arise, we advise on enforcement options and, where necessary, pursue recovery through UAE courts or arbitral tribunals.

Key Practice Areas

  • Technology licensing and SaaS agreements

  • Cross-border trade documentation and Incoterms advisory
  • Force majeure and contract risk assessment
  • Contract enforcement and breach of contract claims
  • Non-disclosure and confidentiality agreements
