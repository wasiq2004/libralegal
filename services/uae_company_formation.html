UAE Company Formation

Establishing companies across mainland UAE, free zones, and offshore jurisdictions — from jurisdiction selection and licensing to visa processing and compliance.

Overview

The UAE offers one of the world's most business-friendly environments for company incorporation—spanning a mainland licensing framework, over 40 free zones with sector-specific regulatory regimes, and offshore structures in Jebel Ali Free Zone (JAFZA) and Ras Al Khaimah. Choosing the right structure for your business requires careful analysis of ownership, activity, tax, and operational factors.

Libra Legal Consultancy guides investors and entrepreneurs through the full process of company establishment in the UAE, from initial jurisdiction selection through to licensing, shareholder documentation, visa processing, bank account opening support, and ongoing regulatory compliance. We advise individuals, families, and corporates on structuring their UAE presence in a manner that is legally sound, operationally efficient, and commercially optimal.

Strategic Approach

We take a structured approach to company formation—beginning with a thorough analysis of the client's business activity, ownership preferences, target market, and compliance obligations. We then present a clear comparison of suitable jurisdiction options, including cost, timeline, restrictions, and tax treatment, enabling our clients to make an informed decision before committing resources.

Once the structure is determined, we manage the incorporation process end-to-end, coordinating with the relevant licensing authority, drafting corporate documents, and liaising with government departments to achieve smooth and timely establishment.
