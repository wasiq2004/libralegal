Aviation & Air Transport Law

Advisory on regulatory compliance, aircraft financing, insurance, and liability issues for airlines, operators, and aviation service providers in the UAE and GCC.

Overview

Libra Legal Consultancy advises clients on aviation & air transport law matters with a practical focus on commercial outcomes, procedural clarity, and risk control in the UAE. Whether the instruction involves regulatory navigation, transaction support, or contentious proceedings, we help clients move forward with advice that is structured, responsive, and aligned to the realities of the local market.

Our work typically spans early-stage assessment, document review, stakeholder coordination, and implementation support. We tailor each engagement to the client's sector, operating footprint, and urgency, while keeping the broader legal and regulatory context firmly in view.

Strategic Approach

Our approach begins with a close review of the client's objectives, the governing legal framework, and the pressure points most likely to affect timing, cost, or exposure. From there, we build a clear action plan that prioritises the immediate issues without losing sight of the longer-term commercial position.

Where a matter becomes contentious or regulator-facing, we combine careful preparation with decisive execution. We work alongside management teams, investors, in-house counsel, and external experts as needed, so that the advice remains coordinated and commercially useful at every stage.
