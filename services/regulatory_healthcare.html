Regulatory & Healthcare Law

Compliance advisory for the UAE's regulated healthcare sector.

Overview

Libra Legal Consultancy's regulatory and healthcare practice advises the full breadth of participants in the UAE's healthcare system—from individual practitioners and specialist clinics to hospital groups, insurers, and pharmaceutical companies. Our team has deep familiarity with the regulatory frameworks operated by Dubai Health Authority (DHA), Ministry of Health and Prevention (MOHAP), Department of Health Abu Dhabi (DOH), and the Health Care City Authority (HCML), as well as the Central Bank of UAE's insurance regulatory framework as it intersects with medical liability.

We assist clients in navigating licensing requirements, regulatory investigations, malpractice claims, and the complex interplay between UAE federal health regulation and emirate-level frameworks. Our advisory is precise, commercially aware, and delivered with the urgency that healthcare regulatory matters demand.

Strategic Approach

Healthcare regulation in the UAE is multi-layered and dynamic. Our approach is to provide clients with a clear map of their regulatory environment—identifying licensing obligations, reporting requirements, and compliance risks before they become problems. Where clients face investigations or enforcement action, we act swiftly and strategically to protect their regulatory standing and professional reputation.

We combine regulatory expertise with an understanding of the clinical context, enabling us to communicate effectively with regulators and to present our clients' positions with authority and precision. In malpractice and liability matters, we work closely with medical experts to build robust defensive strategies.
