About Libra Legal Consultancy

A UAE-Based Specialist Legal Practice

Libra Legal Consultancy is a UAE-based legal consultancy providing specialist advisory and dispute resolution services across healthcare, insurance, commercial, and regulated sectors.

We advise clients on complex legal and regulatory matters within the UAE, with a particular focus on healthcare regulation, insurance disputes, contractual risk, and structured legal enforcement.

Our practice is defined by regulatory precision, technical legal understanding, and a structured approach to dispute management. We support clients throughout the entire lifecycle of legal risk.

We act for healthcare professionals, institutions, corporates, insurers, and individuals requiring clear, reliable, and strategically aligned legal guidance.
